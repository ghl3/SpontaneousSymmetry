Work

LendUp

LendUp is a startup that aims to safely offer credit all who need it. We currently offer credit cards, installment loans, and single payment loans to those who qualify. I have been at the company since early 2013, when I was the 11th employee and first data scientist. Throughout my time at LendUp, I have worked on many of different aspects of the company, including credit modeling, fraud mitigation, data pipelines, and internal tooling.

Atlas

During my PhD, I worked on the ATLAS experiment, which is one of the two major projects at the Large Hadron Collider. I conducted my research on Top Quarks, using modern statistical techniques and technologies to measure the it's properties. I also developed statistical modeling packages to enable other researchers to better model their data and make more accurate inferences from it. For a longer discussion of the experiment and my research, see here.
